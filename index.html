The Blazing Firefox Logos

The firefox logo depicts a bright blazing fox with a fiery tail of:

  • red
  • orange
  • yellow

flames igniting through its body. The glowing fox envelops around
the bottom of our beautiful blue earth in a brilliant flaming design.
If you look closely, you can see several shapes outlining along the
earth that possibly represent the continents that we reside on and
encompass our earth.

Another firefox logo that instead depicts a white silhouette of a fox
with a fiery tail, as it resides upon an orange three-dimensional
hexagon. This fox may be less detailed than our first logo, but it
appears to have very similar features, such as:

  1. the sharp layered tail at the bottom of the fox
  2. curved body along the middle
  3. the outreaching paw
  4. the similarly structured upright paw

However, this fox is portrayed instead as a silhouette on an orange
hexagon, rather than our blue earth.
