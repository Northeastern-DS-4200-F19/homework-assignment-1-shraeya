The Blazing Firefox Logos

The firefox logo depicts a bright blazing fox with a fiery tail of:

  • red
  • orange
  • yellow

flames igniting through its body. The glowing fox envelops around the bottom of our beautiful blue earth in a brilliant flaming design. If you look closely, you can see several shapes outlining along the earth that possibly represent the continents that we reside on and comprise our earth. Looking at the fox's features starting from bottom of the body to the top you can see that it has a:

  1. sharp layered tail at the bottom
  2. a curved body along the middle
  3. an outreaching paw towards the top
  4. an upright head at the top

All of this encompasses the Firefox logo that we see before us.
